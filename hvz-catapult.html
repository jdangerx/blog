JOHN XIA IS ON THE INTERNET

HvZ Catapult

Tue 20 May 2014

For my last game of Humans vs. Zombies I decided that I would do the dumbest thing possible. So I went to the wood shop, found some scrap, and built a back-mounted catapult that completely ineffectively launched socks. It was powered by HvZ bandanas from previous games. When that proved a little too pathetic I also added some rubber bands. You may notice that this was also when Twitch was Playing Pokemon Red.

Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.
